Privacy Policy for Crazy Car

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

Welcome to Crazy Car. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

1. No Account or Login Required

Crazy Car does not require a user account, registration, or login to play. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information (PII) during the gameplay experience.

While we do not collect personal data, the game may process certain non-personal technical data to ensure a smooth experience:

  • Device Information: We may access basic device information (model, OS version) to optimize performance.
  • Gameplay Data: Local storage is used to save your high scores and unlocked items directly on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

Our game may use third-party services (such as Google Play Services or AdMob) that may collect information used to identify you. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob

4. Children’s Privacy

Crazy Car does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no login is required, we do not track or store identity-based data for any users, including children under 13.

5. Data Security

As we do not store personal data on our servers, your information is as secure as your mobile device. We recommend keeping your device OS updated to the latest version.
